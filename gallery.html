DigitalKalakar

Born and raised in Bihar, I now reside in Pune, Maharashtra. With a keen eye for detail, color, and natural light, I create original imagery with a true sense of place for clients across the genres of Weddings, Portraits, Architecture, and Landscapes. The need to travel was instilled in me at a young age. Growing up in a family of artists and travelers, I'm grateful to have been immersed in an environment of creativity and curiosity from the start. Before I learned how to make a photograph, I learned how to be a traveler. Travel is when I'm most engaged, most, most challenged. Learning bits of a new language and culture, getting lost in winding cobblestone streets or narrow dirt paths, meeting local people, and taking in every element of a moment sights, scents, sounds and feelings. This is when I'm most alive. The camera allows me to capture and share these moments. I'm also a Photographer + Business Manager at Future Frames Studios, Pune.
